Projects

In my projects you'll find an eclectic span of interests, but I've highlighted some of my personal favorites which I believe provide some novel utility to the world.


Novel Cell Type for Cancer Diagnosis Prediction

My project w/ Professor Tao He at her lab in UCSF and SFSU. This project was made possible by funding given by the National Science Foundation's LEAPS award. The project aimed to explore various machine learning methods for predicting cell types using novel TCR data to expedite cancer diagnoses. During the project, I had the opportunity to train multiple statistical models using modern machine learning techniques.


Conduits

Conduits started as a question of how would I redesign a classroom which was integrated with LLM's. For me this has become a special tool that will help catch students when they begin to get behind, and keep them motivated to continue learning while keeping the teacher in the loop the entire time.


ASCII-AI

AI voices need a face, but AI faces currently are still too much in the uncanny valley. ASCII-AI provides an ASCII rendered face to your AI assistant.


MemDex

MemDex is my way of combining LLM's and Anki to help my own working memory be the best it can be. Select any piece of text that you're reading and MemDex will create the Anki card for you.


Mushroom Classifier

A fun project for a Catagorical Data Analysis class where we used the R language exclusively.
